HealthCare Plus - Website Planning Document

Site Name

Site Name: HealthCare Plus

This name was chosen because the website is focused on providing reliable medical information, healthcare tips, and connecting patients with healthcare professionals. It conveys trust and well-being.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote health awareness, provide medical guidance, and make healthcare more accessible online. It will include:

  • Articles on common medical conditions, prevention, and treatment
  • Guides on healthy living, nutrition, and fitness
  • Doctor and hospital directories with contact details
  • Appointment booking and telemedicine options

Scenarios

  • Where can I find trusted information about symptoms I am experiencing?
  • How do I book an online consultation with a doctor near me?
  • What healthy meal plans can I follow to manage my diabetes?

Color Schema

  • Blue (#0066cc): Used for main headings, navigation, and buttons. Represents trust and professionalism.
  • Teal (#009688): Used for highlights, icons, and call-to-action elements. Symbolizes health and balance.
  • White (#ffffff): Background and content areas for a clean, clinical look.

Typography

  • Font: Poppins (Google Font)
  • Usage: Used for all headings and body text for readability and modern style across devices.
